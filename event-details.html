Event Overview

Experience a lively and dynamic celebration at the Adwa Zero Kilometer Museum Project, immersed in the rich tapestry of Ethiopian culture. The event promises an enchanting showcase of traditional Ethiopian music and dance, creating an atmosphere of jubilant festivity. Attendees will have the opportunity to witness captivating historical reenactments that vividly bring to life the heroic tales of Ethiopian forefathers who triumphed at the Battle of Adwa against Mussolini’s Italy. Immerse yourself in the essence of Ethiopian heritage through mesmerizing performances and a celebration of the nation's resilience.
